About Me

Hey there! I'm Freshia Kaye, a 20-year-old graphic designer based in Davao Del Sur. Creativity has always been my driving force, whether I'm sketching in my notebook, experimenting with digital art, or exploring new design trends. Professionally, I'm passionate about crafting visually compelling stories and helping businesses stand out in a crowded digital world. With a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and two years of industry experience, I bring a blend of artistic flair and strategic thinking to every project. When I'm not immersed in design projects, you can find me hiking in the mountains, Sketching some buildings. I believe in the power of kindness, authenticity, and continuous learning. Whether it's mastering a new design technique or exploring a new hiking trail, I'm always up for a challenge.
